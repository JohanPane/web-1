What is UNAI ?

Universitas Advent Indonesia(UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by the Seventh-day
Adventist Church of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that it was founded
in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleidinf School der Advent Zending. In the past,
this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
